About Food Rescue AI

Saving food. Serving humanity. Powered by smart technology.

Food Waste Problem

Millions of tons of food are wasted every day while people go hungry.

AI Solution

Our platform uses intelligent logic to match food with nearby NGOs.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and help those in need efficiently.

What We Do

Food Rescue AI connects hotels and food donors with NGOs in real time. Using distance-based smart suggestions, NGOs can quickly identify food available within their preferred range.

This ensures that food reaches the right people at the right time, reducing waste and maximizing impact.

Our goal is to create a smarter, faster, and more efficient food rescue system.

How It Works

1. Hotels register and upload available food.

2. NGOs log in and view available food.

3. AI suggests food based on preferred distance.

4. NGOs claim food and arrange pickup.

Key Features

  • Distance-based AI suggestions
  • Real-time food availability updates
  • Simple and fast claiming process
  • Secure login for NGOs and Hotels
  • Efficient food distribution system

Why Choose Food Rescue AI?

Unlike traditional systems, Food Rescue AI focuses on smart matching using location and distance preferences. This reduces delay and ensures that food reaches NGOs before expiry.

Our Vision

We aim to build a future where no food is wasted and no one sleeps hungry. By using technology, we want to create a fast and reliable food rescue network.
